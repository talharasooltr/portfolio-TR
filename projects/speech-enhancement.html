Speech Enhancement Pipeline

A multi-stage AI system that takes raw, imperfect audio and outputs polished, clear speech — by chaining automatic transcription, LLM-powered text refinement, and neural text-to-speech synthesis.

Role

Full-Stack AI Developer

Timeline

6 weeks

Stack

Python, Whisper, Gemini API, TTS, FastAPI

Problem

Content creators, educators, and accessibility teams frequently work with audio that contains grammatical errors, filler words, accent-heavy pronunciation, or poor recording quality. Manual editing is time-consuming and requires specialized skills.

Approach

The pipeline processes audio through three sequential stages:

  • Transcription: OpenAI Whisper converts raw audio to text with high accuracy, handling multiple accents and background noise gracefully.
  • Text Enhancement: The transcript is passed to Gemini LLM with a carefully engineered prompt that corrects grammar, removes filler words, improves sentence structure, and preserves the speaker's original intent and tone.
  • Speech Regeneration: The enhanced text is fed into a neural TTS engine that produces natural-sounding speech, with configurable voice, speed, and tone parameters.

Outcome

The system processes a 10-minute audio clip in under 45 seconds. User testing showed a 92% preference rate for the enhanced output over the original across a panel of 50 listeners. The pipeline is deployed as a REST API, enabling integration into existing content workflows.

Key Technologies

  • OpenAI Whisper — Speech-to-text transcription
  • Gemini LLM — Contextual text enhancement and grammar correction
  • Neural TTS — High-fidelity speech synthesis
  • FastAPI — REST API serving layer
  • Python — Pipeline orchestration
